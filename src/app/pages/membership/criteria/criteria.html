Membership Criteria

ECAI maintains strict quality standards. Find out if you qualify to join India's most prestigious association for education consultants.

1

Experience Requirements

The applicant must have a minimum of 3 years of experience in the field of education consulting and student recruitment.

2

Office Infrastructure

Proof of a physical office with professional interior and necessary infrastructure for student counseling.

3

Ethical Track Record

A clean professional history with no complaints from students or partner institutions regarding unethical practices.

4

Institutional Tie-ups

The applicant should represent at least 5 recognized international or domestic educational institutions.
